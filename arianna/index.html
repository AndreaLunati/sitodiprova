Our Story

Jacksons Dairy was born from a family tradition of love for the land and its animals. Our farm, located in the lush hills of Lombardy, has been crafting premium dairy products for over 30 years.

Farm Fresh. Family Owned.

We use only milk from cows we raise ourselves, ensuring full control over quality and freshness. Our cheese and yogurt are processed with care, using traditional methods and no preservatives.
